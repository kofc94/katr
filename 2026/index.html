An Exciting Tradition

What is K'night at the Races?

Join us at Heritage Hall (177 Bedford St, Lexington) for an exciting community gala! Place your bets and cheer on your favorite horses projected onto the big screen with full video and audio commentary.

🏇

5 Action Races

Watch thoroughbred horse races on the giant screen! Bet on your favorites with play money and win real cash & raffle prizes.

🍽️

Buffet Dinner & Drinks

Enjoy a delicious catered dinner, snacks, signature Derby drinks, beer, and wine at our cash bar.

🎨

Kid's Activity Table

Fun crafts, games, and creative activities designed specifically for children and families attending the event.

🎁

Silent Auction & Raffles

Bid on luxury gift baskets, sports memorabilia, restaurant gift certificates, and win 50/50 cash raffles.

Easy Step-by-Step Guide

How The Evening Works

1️⃣

Arrive & Check In

Doors open at 6:00 PM at Heritage Hall (177 Bedford St, Lexington). Pick up your official program and explore the venue.

2️⃣

Enjoy Buffet Dinner

Relax and enjoy a hot buffet dinner with friends, family, and fellow community members before the races begin.

3️⃣

Place Your Wagers

Wagering windows open before each of the 5 races. Place $2 bets on any horse in the field to boost your odds of winning!

4️⃣

Cheer & Collect Winnings

Cheer on your horses on the big screen. Winning tickets collect cash payouts right after each race result is declared official!
